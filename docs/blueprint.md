# **App Name**: LogiFlow

## Core Features:

- Intelligent Operations Dashboard: Visual overview of total deliveries, gross revenue, and driver payouts using sleek bento-grid metric cards.
- AI WhatsApp Extraction Tool: An AI-powered tool that reasons through unstructured WhatsApp chat logs to automatically extract delivery addresses, zones, and standard fees.
- Fleet & Driver Management: Comprehensive directory of active drivers with real-time status tracking, vehicle info, and contact details.
- Store Connectivity Suite: Centralized management of logistic units and satellite stores with custom fee structures and contact integration.
- Operational Detailing: Real-time logging of delivery transactions with advanced filtering, status badges, and map location visualization.
- Logistics Heat Mapping: High-density data visualization showing delivery demand clusters across geographical delivery zones.
- Persistent Logistics Store: Unified database to persist store metadata, driver availability, and historical delivery logs.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Mint Emerald (#4edea3) used for main actions and branding to evoke efficiency.
- Background color: Deep Onyx (#131315), a rich dark surface providing a high-tech agency feel.
- Accent color: Cyan Frost (#4cd7f6) used for data trends and highlight indicators, creating a tech-centric analogous harmony.
- The 'Inter' font is recommended for both headlines and body text to achieve a modern, objective, and machined appearance. Its high legibility at small sizes is ideal for dense operational data.
- Utilize 'Material Symbols Outlined' with a consistent light-weighting (grade 0, weight 400) to maintain a minimal, geometric professional look.
- A sophisticated sidebar-based navigation with 'Glassmorphism' panels featuring a blur of 12px for modern depth.
- Subtle entry transitions including 'slide-in-from-bottom' for data cards and gentle 'scale' micro-interactions on button clicks.
